ARAM (or All Random All Mid) is a game mode in League of Legends in a 5v5 format on the. Howling Abyss with the objective to destroy the opposing team's nexus. It was released on June 29, 2012. Players are given random champions from the available champion pool and are able to trade or reroll their picks.
